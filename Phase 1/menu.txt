Joystick Commands

Left Stick:
Up/down: Move the robot forwards and back
Left/right: turn the robot left/right 

Right Axis:
Up/down: Move the camera left and right
Up/down: Move the camera up and down

Start: Home the System

L1: Open the image stream
L2: Close the image stream

R1: Record audio for DTMF processing

A: Loop A demonstration
B: Pivot 180 degrees around the right wheel
X: Take a picture for shape detection
Y: Loop D demonstration
